Cubicflow Case Study

Upscale gastropub and craft beer locale in Uptown Whittier

The Challenge

The Bottle Room serves up culinary flare and rare craft beers off the eclectic Greenleaf Avenue in Uptown Whittier. The Bottle Room is also an established and extremely well-liked business, so extra care was given to tackling their digital re-brand and new website, staying true to what makes The Bottle Room great while opening the doors to a new generation of customers.

Development
Brand Identity
Print Design

How Cubicflow Helped

As part of The Bottle Room's rebrand we designed a new logo and typography system, various print collateral, package design and glasswear. We also built their new website and online menu system, while producing a ton of photography to showcase Chef Alcazar's delicious menu and craft-beer finds. Seasonally, we help The Bottle Room launch their new menu and the special events the restaurant hosts.
