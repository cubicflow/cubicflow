Cubicflow Case Study

The only event where kids can experience all the many trucks they love — in the driver’s seat.

The Challenge

Truck Adventures wanted to up their game big-time to celebrate their 2nd annual event. They knew that growing exposure, building a brand, and really solidifying their web presence would all be key to increasing ticket sales. Our combined efforts led to an increase in over 400% in ticket sales with soaring conversion rates.

Web Design
Brand Identity
Ecommerce

How Cubicflow Helped

We created a refreshed corporate identity and logomark - along with a series of fun illustrations - to launch alongside their completely new website and e-commerce ticketing system. We then turned to targeted social media campaigns that funneled people into the site for more info or to purchase tickets.
