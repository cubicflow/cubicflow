Life Flower

Client case study

The Challenge

Harlowe's - a boutique restaurant located in historic Old Pasadena, CA - first approached us early in their business planning to work on the design concept. The Neue Deco-inspired concept that followed helped close a deal, start a new business, and Cubicflow has since worked on the Harlowe's brand identity, web design, and much more.

Brand Identity
Print Design

How Cubicflow Helped

Harlowe's got our full-service treatment. Logo design and branding, print collateral, completely responsive custom web site, and more. Collaboration played a vital role in creating a brand where none existed before. Every detail was considered -- when the Harlowe's team painstakingly restored the building's original wood interior, we partnered with a local and classically-trained sign painter to bring to life the signage and gold leaf window designs.
