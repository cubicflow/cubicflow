Cubicflow Case Study

New restaurant in the heart of Old Pasadena

The Challenge

Located in historic Old Pasadena, CA, the handsome wood Art Deco interior of Harlowe’s French Dip surrounds and transports you back in time. Harlowe's first approached us early in their business planning to work on the design concept. After producing the initial Neue Deco-inspired concept -- used to secure the restaurant's investment -- Cubicflow worked on the entire brand identity, web design, and more for Harlowe's.

How Cubicflow Helped

Harlowe's got our full-service treatment. Logo design and branding, print collateral, completely responsive custom web site, and more. While the Harlowe's team painstakingly restored the original wood interior, we partnered with a local, classically-trained sign painter to bring to life the signage and gold leaf window designs.
