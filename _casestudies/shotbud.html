Shotbud

Client case study

The Challenge

The work we did with Shotbud leaned into our agile and integrated approaches to development and design. On one hand, our job was to help build a brand from the ground up, which required both a certain sensitivity and good listening skills. On the other hand, our development efforts were to not just build an iOS app or an Apple Watch app, but to create a harmonious user experience that is easy to use and takes advantage of the Apple Watch’s sensor capabilities.

How Cubicflow Helped

Working in any new product area presents a number of challenges and opportunities to create great design. The privilege to build the app as well as develop its communication meant every part could feel all the more familiar and immediately understandable to the user, key when you want to eliminate friction in technology. Shotbud arrives in the Apple App Store in early 2018.
